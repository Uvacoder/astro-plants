Hello!

Welcome to Spotted, a site about rad houseplants and how to care for them.

This website is a personal project created by Jack Guinane and Ella Minicola. The original intent was to provide a simple blog that would allow us to share cool houseplants we spotted with each other. While on a walk through Grange Park, we decided that we also wanted to build a catalog of plants, each accompanied by a custom watercolor illustration. The final result was this website, developed over the summer of 2018.

Ella Minicola is mostly into ocean life. She loves diving but occasionally emerges from the sea to paint her houseplants.

Jack Guinane is a 3rd-year Computer Science student at Queen's University. He enjoys design-driven product development and has always been "this way"& about plants.
