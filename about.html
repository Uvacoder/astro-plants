Hello!

Welcome to Spotted, a site about rad house plants and how to care for them.

This website is a personal project created by Jack Guinane and Ella Minicola. The orignal goal was to provide a simple blog that would allow us to share cool house plants and green spaces we spotted with each other. While on a walk through Grange Park, we decieded that we wanted to build a catalog of house plants too, each acompanied by a custom watercolour illustration.
